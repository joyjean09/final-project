There are three teams: Team Instinct, Team Valor, and Team Mystic Each team is represented by a color and insignia based on one of the legendary birds.

Team Instinct is represented by the color yellow and values intuition in Pokemon. The team’s insignia is based on the electric bird, Zapdos.
Team Valor is represented by red and values power in Pokemon. The team’s insignia is based on the legendary fire bird, Moltres.
Team Mystic is represented by the color blue and values wisdom in Pokemon. The team’s insignia is based on the legendary ice bird, Articuno.
